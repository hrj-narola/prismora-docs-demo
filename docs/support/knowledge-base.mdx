# Knowledge Base

Prismora’s Knowledge Base helps connect your team’s existing information (policies, specs, prior decisions, documentation) to the support and automation workflows.

:::tip When to use Knowledge Base
Use it when you want Prismora to answer and generate tests using your team’s terminology, decisions, and constraints (not just generic information).
:::

## What it’s for

- Provide context for analysis and test generation
- Enable consistent answers and terminology across your team
- Reduce repeated manual explanations

## What to add

- Product requirements and specs
- Engineering guidelines and best practices
- Design notes and decision records
- Common edge cases and known limitations

## Typical flow

1. Open your Knowledge Base settings
2. Add or connect content sources (files, links, or connected documents—depending on your setup)
3. Verify the content is indexed/available
4. Use the Knowledge Base when generating tests or answering questions

## Recommended sources

- Product requirements and specs
- Engineering guidelines and best practices
- Decision records and prior investigation notes
- Known edge cases and limitations

## Troubleshooting

- If content doesn’t appear to be used, confirm that you added it to the correct workspace/project scope.
- If answers look outdated, check content freshness and re-run/update indexing if supported.
