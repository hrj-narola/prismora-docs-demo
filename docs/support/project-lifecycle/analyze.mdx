# Platform Analysis & Project Summary

As you feed information to the Chatbot, the right-side panel will automatically transition to an **AI-generated project analysis**. The platform synthesizes your data into organized compartments.

## Analysis Components

- **Authorizations**: The system automatically detects if external connections or specific integrations are required for your workflows (e.g., "No Connections Required").
- **Project Summary**: A polished, AI-authored overview detailing your application's use-cases, primary features, and target audience. 
- **Tech Stack**: The AI detects and enumerates your core technologies (e.g., HTML, MySQL, custom modules, external APIs).

## Review and Refine via Chatbot

The initial generated analysis acts as a living document:

- Review the Summary, Authorizations, and Tech Stack ensuring everything aligns with your actual platform.
- If you notice a missing third-party integration or an incorrectly summarized flow, **simply tell the chatbot**. 
- The AI will digest your feedback and instantly update the Project Summary to reflect the correct state.
