# Project Initialization

When you initiate a new project, you will be greeted by the Prismora AI Assistant on the "What would you like to test today?" screen. This is the first step in helping the AI understand your project.

## Starting the Conversation

The AI Assistant provides a welcoming message outlining the types of context it can process, such as documentation, design files, and database details.

To kick off the onboarding process, you can:
1. **Choose a primary focus area**: Click on one of the quick-start tags such as **Context**, **Code**, **Design**, or **Requirements** to guide the AI's initial setup.
2. **Describe your project**: Use the text input at the bottom to briefly explain your application, target audience, or what specifically you want to test.

## Proceeding to the Chatbot

Once you enter your initial description and submit your response, you will be automatically redirected to the comprehensive split-screen AI Chatbot interface. From there, you can upload specific files, converse further, and review the AI's generated analysis.
