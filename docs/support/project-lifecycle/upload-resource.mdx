# Upload Resource & Share Data

To generate accurate and comprehensive test cases, the AI needs context. Sharing your data is incredibly intuitive.

## Sharing Methods

- **Upload Files**: You can uniquely select and upload functional requirements, mockups, or architectural diagrams (e.g., `.docx` files, PDFs, images).
- **Direct Input**: You can manually type text into the chat, allowing you to explain distinct user flows, testing boundaries, or edge cases.

## Resource Processing

Upon receiving your files and input, the AI instantly acknowledges receipt and begins learning your project's architecture seamlessly in the background.
