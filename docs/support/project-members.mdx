# Project Members & Invites

This page covers how to manage who can access your Prismora project and how to invite additional members.

:::note Roles may vary
Different Prismora deployments may show different role names, but the goal stays the same: control who can view, edit, and run testing.
:::

## Project Members

### What “Project Members” typically controls

- Who can view and/or edit project configuration
- Who can upload resources, run analysis, and execute test runs
- Who can generate test cases and manage results actions

### Roles (typical)

- `Admin`: manage configuration, integrations, and permissions
- `Member`: contribute resources and run analysis/testing
- `Viewer` (or equivalent): view results and documentation

### Typical steps

1. Open your project
2. Navigate to the **Project Members** area
3. Review current members and their roles/permissions

## Invite Members

### Typical steps

1. In **Project Members**, choose **Invite Members**
2. Enter the invitee’s email (or select from your org directory, depending on your setup)
3. Choose the appropriate role/permission level
4. Send the invite
5. Confirm the invite appears in the members list

### Invite checklist

- [ ] Invite email matches the person’s work email
- [ ] Selected role matches their responsibilities
- [ ] Invite is accepted successfully

### Troubleshooting

- If someone can’t access the project after accepting, verify their email was invited correctly and that they have an assigned role.
