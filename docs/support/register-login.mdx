# Authentication

This page covers the standard account flows for Prismora. Getting started is fast and secure, allowing your team to focus on building great products while intelligent, AI-powered test automation does the heavy lifting.

:::tip At a glance
Registering for a Prismora account takes just minutes. After logging in, you can start creating and configuring projects using our real-time analytics and enterprise-grade secure platform.
:::

## Create Your Account (Sign Up)

Begin your journey to transform testing into innovation. You only need to register once to gain access to automated test suites and actionable insights.

### Registration Steps

1. Navigate to the Prismora application and click on **Sign up** to access the "Create your account" page.
2. Enter your **Full Name** and **Email Address**.
3. Create a strong, secure **Password**.
4. By creating an account, you agree to our **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**. 
5. Click **Create Account**.

*Alternative Approach:* You can easily bypass manual forms by clicking **Continue with Google** for a deeply integrated and fast registration.

---

## Email Verification (OTP)

Prismora provides enterprise security, including bank-grade encryption and compliance with SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA. To maintain this security, all new users signing up via email must verify their identity.

### Verification Steps

1. Check your email inbox. We will send a secure 6-digit confirmation code to your registered email address.
2. On the **Verify Your Email** screen, input the 6-digit code.
3. Click the **Verify Email** button to complete the process.

**Troubleshooting**
- If you made a typo during sign up, you can click **Back to Register** to start over.
- If the code has not arrived after a few minutes, click **Resend Code**.

---

## Login

Welcome back! Sign in to continue to your dashboard, track test performance, and manage your user flows.

### Login Steps

1. On the Prismora **Sign In** screen, enter your registered **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **Sign In**.

*Alternative Approach:* If your account is linked with Google, click **Continue with Google** for single-sign-on access.

---

## Forgot Password & Reset Password

If you lose access to your account, you can quickly recover it using our secure OTP-based password reset flow.

### Recovery Steps

1. Navigate to the Prismora **Sign In** screen.
2. Click the **Forgot password?** link located above the Sign In button.
3. Submit the **Email Address** associated with your Prismora account. You will be redirected to an OTP verification screen.
4. Check your email inbox for a secure 6-digit confirmation code.
5. Enter the correct OTP into the verification screen. Once successfully verified, you will be automatically redirected to the Reset Password page.
6. Create and confirm your new, secure password.
7. Return to the **Sign In** screen and log in with your newly created credentials.
