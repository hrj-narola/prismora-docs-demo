# Register & Login

This page covers the standard account flow for Prismora: register, login, and move into your first workspace.

:::tip At a glance
You only need this once per person. After login, you can start creating and configuring projects.
:::

## Register

### Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- Your organization/workspace details (if applicable)

### Register checklist

- [ ] Choose a strong password (follow your org rules)
- [ ] Use a correct email (for verification)
- [ ] Complete any verification prompt if shown

### Steps

1. Open Prismora and choose **Register**
2. Enter your details and create your password
3. Submit the form to create your account
4. Verify your email (if prompted)

### Expected result

- Your account is created and you can proceed to login.

### Troubleshooting

- If email verification is required, check your inbox/spam and re-send the verification email if available.
- If registration fails, confirm your email format and that your password meets the required rules.

## Login

### Login checklist

- [ ] Use the same email/username you registered with
- [ ] Complete any verification prompt if shown
- [ ] If you see access denied, confirm your account/org membership

### Steps

1. Open Prismora and choose **Login**
2. Enter your email/username and password
3. Complete any additional verification (if prompted)

### Expected result

- You land in your Prismora dashboard with access to your projects and features.
